Cardamom Powder

Cardamom powder is a spice derived from the seeds of the cardamom plant. With a strong, aromatic flavor, it is widely used in both sweet and savory dishes, beverages, and desserts. Known for its digestive properties, cardamom powder adds a warm, slightly citrusy taste to culinary creations. It is a staple in many cuisines, contributing depth and complexity to various recipes.

Bath Robes

Bath robes are cozy, absorbent garments worn after bathing or lounging. Typically made from cotton, terrycloth, or luxurious fabrics like silk, they provide comfort and warmth. Available in various styles, including kimono, shawl collar, and hooded designs, bath robes often feature pockets and a tie belt. Popular in spas and homes, they offer a stylish and practical solution for relaxation and post-shower comfort.
