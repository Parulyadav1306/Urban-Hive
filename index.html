Cardamom Powder

Cardamom powder is a spice derived from the seeds of the cardamom plant. With a strong, aromatic flavor, it is widely used in both sweet and savory dishes, beverages, and desserts. Known for its digestive properties, cardamom powder adds a warm, slightly citrusy taste to culinary creations. It is a staple in many cuisines, contributing depth and complexity to various recipes.

Red Chilli Powder

Red chilli powder is a spice derived from dried, ground red chillies. It adds heat and flavor to various cuisines globally. Rich in capsaicin, it stimulates taste buds and offers potential health benefits, such as metabolism boost and pain relief. Used in diverse dishes, from curries to marinades, its vibrant color and pungent taste make it a staple in many kitchens.

Turmeric Powder

Turmeric powder, derived from the Curcuma longa plant, is a vibrant yellow spice widely used in culinary and medicinal applications. Renowned for its earthy flavor and distinctive color, it contains curcumin, a compound with anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Commonly found in curry dishes, turmeric is also valued for its potential health benefits, including immune support and anti-inflammatory effects.

Bath Robes

Bath robes are cozy, absorbent garments worn after bathing or lounging. Typically made from cotton, terrycloth, or luxurious fabrics like silk, they provide comfort and warmth. Available in various styles, including kimono, shawl collar, and hooded designs, bath robes often feature pockets and a tie belt. Popular in spas and homes, they offer a stylish and practical solution for relaxation and post-shower comfort.

Bath Sheets

Bath sheets are oversized towels, larger than regular bath towels, typically measuring around 35x60 inches. Designed for maximum coverage and absorbency, they provide a luxurious and spa-like experience after bathing. Made from soft and durable materials like cotton, bath sheets offer a cozy feel while effectively drying the body. Ideal for those who prefer a generously sized and indulgent towel for their daily bathing routine.
